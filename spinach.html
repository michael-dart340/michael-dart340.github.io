SPINACH RECIPE

EASY TO MAKE AND DELICIOUS!

INGREDIENTS

  1. Spinach

STEPS

  1. Take spinach out and place in pan with 2 cups of water.
  2. Turn on stove to about 6 and heat spinach until ready for consumption. Move spinach around in pan.
  3. Enjoy!
